Starlink India prices unveiled

Elon Musk's Starlink has moved a step closer to its commercial launch in India by placing full residential pricing on its local website. The updated page shows the monthly charge at ₹8,600. Reportedly, customers are also required to buy a hardware kit priced at ₹34,000 as a one-time purchase. The plan comes with unlimited data and a 30-day trial window to allow new users enough time to test how the service performs in their area.

During the same month, Starlink also began its initial hiring phase in India, signalling that the company was preparing for a deeper push into the satellite broadband sector.
Starlink, part of Musk's wider space ventures, is among the largest ICT firms worldwide and operates the biggest fleet of communication satellites
